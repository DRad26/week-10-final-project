Suntory Yamazaki Distillery
Asia

Yamazaki® Whisky is Suntory's flagship single malt whisky, from Japan’s first and oldest malt distillery. Multi-layered with fruit and Mizunara aromas, its signature taste is highly praised by whisky connoisseurs all over the world. Today, Suntory Single Malt Whisky Yamazaki® Whisky is not only the No.1 single malt whisky in Japan, but is enjoyed in more than 35 countries.
